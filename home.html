About BeLEAF

BeLEAF is created to give the old things you want to throw away a second life. It not only allows people to find non-profit shops to donate their item to, but also enables them to upload their products on BeLEAF to donate to buyers. The latter option allows a intimate connection to form between the buyers and donors. BeLEAF exists as a platform for people who are financially limited, to shop for free or very cheap items. As the old saying goes, "one man's trash is another's gold". We hope that through this website people will find happiness in life.
